Who Are We?

We are a boutique HR Consulting firm committed to serve our clients in addressing their Hiring Requirements.

What do we offer?

Our role is to work with employers and talents to facilitate a successful match. This can range from advising a global company on a candidate sourcing strategy to helping a job seeker find their dream job. Our Process model is based on the beliefs of being positive and approachable, enabling us to constantly get better candidate quality and decrease the time-to-hire, at the same time lower the Clients’ cost.

What’s Unique?

In-depth understanding of client’s requirement including the organizational structure and environment. We foster open communication and transparency with both - clients and candidates, having equal concern for their interest, ensuring long term association. We focus on finding the right talent, who emulate the company's culture and possess the appropriate competencies and skills
